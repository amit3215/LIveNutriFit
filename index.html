Free Food

Free food is any food or drink that contains fewer than 20 calories or under 5 grams of Carbohydrate per serving. You can eat as much as you want of items that have no serving size specified. You may eat two or three servings per day of those items that have a specific serving size. Be sure to spread them out through the day. Maintain moderation in consumption.

List of Partially Free Foods (Limited Intake, 3 Serving per Day)
